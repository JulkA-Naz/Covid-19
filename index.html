About Coronavirus

According to the tribute of the World Health Organization, 80% of people who became infected carry the disease in an asymptomatic or mild form, although they do not care for a severe cold and do not suffer from a specific infection. Approximately 15% of patients develop an important form of illness, if necessary, sour therapy, and 5% have an extremely important form, which means great attention in the minds of intensive care.

Remember that for the treatment of coronavirus infection, self-acceptance and taking antibiotics is categorically obligatory, except that viruses, in addition, including coronavirus, are not sensitive to antibiotics.

The best way to protect yourself and your loved ones from COVID-19 is to get vaccinated and boosted when eligible, follow testing guidelines, wear a mask, wash your hands and practice physical distancing.
